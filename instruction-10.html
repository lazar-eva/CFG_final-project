Vegan "Egg" Salad Sandwich

Egg salad sandwiches are a classic lunch or dinner, and we've decided to veganize it. It's an incredibly easy and speedy recipe to whip together, and the best part is that this recipe is perfect for meal prep. Enjoy it on your sandwiches or wraps throughout the week!

Ingredients

  • 11.4 oz (325 g) extra firm tofu
  • ½ cup (120 mL) vegan mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbsp (4 g) nutritional yeast flakes
  • 2 Tbsp (6 g) chopped fresh chives
  • 1½ tsp (7 mL) Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp curry powder
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • ¼ tsp kala namak salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 romaine lettuce leaves
  • ½ cucumber, sliced
  • 6 slices whole wheat bread

Directions

  1. Press and drain the tofu, then finely chop it into very small pieces.
  2. Add the tofu to a large bowl, and mix together with the mayonnaise, nutritional yeast, chives, mustard, curry powder, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
  3. Assemble the sandwiches by adding a layer of lettuce, sliced cucumber, and a generous layer of "egg" salad on top of the bread.
  4. Cut in half if desired, and enjoy!

Notes

The "egg" salad can be stored in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
