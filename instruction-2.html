Blueberry Almond Oatmeal

The flavour of sweet blueberries and the nutty taste of almonds are a classic combination found in many delicious baking recipes. We matched these ingredients to make this vibrant purple-coloured oatmeal. It is wholesome, filling, and an absolute treat for the eyes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (81 g) rolled oats
  • 2 cups (480 mL) unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • ½ cup (75 g) frozen blueberries
  • 2 Tbsp (14 g) ground flaxseeds
  • 2 Tbsp (30 mL) maple syrup
  • ½ tsp pure vanilla extract
  • ⅛ tsp almond extract (optional)

Directions

  1. To a saucepan on high heat, add the oats, almond milk, banana, and blueberries. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce to a simmer for 5 minutes, stirring on occasion to prevent burning.
  2. Add the flaxseeds, cooking for another minute. Add more milk if you prefer a thinner consistency, or cook for a couple of minutes longer if you prefer it thicker.
  3. Take the pan off the heat and stir in the maple syrup, vanilla and almond extract.
  4. Divide the oatmeal between the bowls and top with banana slices, fresh blueberries, coconut yogurt, and almond butter. Garnish with sliced almonds and cinnamon if desired and enjoy!

Notes

Store the oatmeal, without toppings, in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 days.
